Planning Your Kitchen Layout

The layout is the foundation of a functional kitchen. Common layouts include:

One-Wall Kitchen: Ideal for small spaces, everything is positioned along a single wall.
Galley Kitchen: Two parallel countertops with a walkway in between. Great for small to medium spaces.
L-Shaped Kitchen: Counters on two perpendicular walls, offering good workflow and plenty of counter space.
U-Shaped Kitchen: Counters on three walls, providing ample storage and work areas. Island Kitchen: Adds an island to any of the above layouts, providing additional workspace and storage.
Peninsula Kitchen: Similar to an island kitchen but the island is connected to one wall.
